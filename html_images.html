Tomorrow by Together, or TXT, is a five-member South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Music in 2019. The group's members are SOOBIN, YEONJUN, BEOMGYU, TAEHYUN, and HUENINGKAI. Their name, which translates to "tomorrow by together," symbolizes the hope that members will come together under one dream to build a better tomorrow. The EP topped the Gaon Album Chart in Korea and the Billboard World Albums Chart in the US, and their debut single "Crown" also hit number one on the World Digital Songs chart. This rapid success led to them winning numerous "New Artist of the Year" awards at major ceremonies.
